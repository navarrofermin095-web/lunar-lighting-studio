FAQ

Questions, answered

Is this a paid position?

It starts as a revenue-share passion project. Payouts come from the studio treasury based on your activity and impact, and standard PayPal payouts are introduced as we scale.

Why do you ask for identity verification?

To keep the team trusted and safe. Documents are handled through a secure verification flow, are never posted publicly or emailed, and are used only to confirm identity. See our Privacy Policy.

I'm under 18 — can I still apply?

Yes, with parental or guardian consent. You'll be asked to provide guardian details during verification.

A role shows as full. Can I still apply?

Absolutely — apply to join the waitlist. Spots open up, and we keep strong applicants on file.

How long until I hear back?

Applications get an AI-assisted first review immediately, and the team follows up on Discord for shortlisted candidates.

Privacy Policy — Identity Verification

What we collect: your application answers, portfolio links, and — for verification — a government or exam document image (and optional selfie).

How documents are handled: verification documents are processed only to confirm your identity through a secure verification flow. They are not posted publicly, shared with third parties for marketing, or sent by email.

Minors: applicants under 18 require verifiable parental/guardian consent, collected during the application.

Retention: verification documents are kept only as long as needed to complete verification, then deleted.

Your rights: you can request access to or deletion of your data at any time by contacting the studio.

This is a template policy. Before going live, replace it with a policy reviewed for your jurisdiction (GDPR / DPDP / etc.).
